# You Alive? _ Round 1

An email service that emails you once a month. When you stop replying we send out an email with information to a designated recipient.

## Test Setup

---

- Ads to landing page
- May 25 — June 8th
- 14 days
- $2 / day
- Audience is currently set to the western world. Avoiding Africa, South America and Asia. But Inlcuding Japan, South Korea and China

This landing page is made before any of the data has come in. 

But the page has benchmark data to beat on the bottom of the page.
