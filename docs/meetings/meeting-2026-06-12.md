### Bonus learning from Jane — conversion ads vs. landing page view ads

Jane ran a parallel experiment (Grant Finder test) comparing two Meta ad optimization targets:

| Metric | Landing page views | Conversion (pixel) |
|---|---|---|
| CTR | ~12-13% | ~1.79% |
| Ad spend | $13 | $5 |
| Reach | high | ~4,000 |
| Email signups | 0 | 9 (out of 27 who landed) |

**Key insight:** Meta groups users into profiles. Conversion-optimized ads reach "likely buyers," not "curious clickers." CTR tanks but actual signups go up.

**Jane's hypothesis:** With a strong concept, it may be possible to convert the curious clickers too. She plans to run a landing page view ad on the rebuilt page to compare apples to apples.

**Current test setup:** Email signup = 20% discount at launch. Not a direct purchase — something to remember when reading conversion data.

**You Alive CTR:** 13% in the early test = one of the highest she's seen (except "Fast in the jar"). Indicates real concept interest.
